# Meetings

Records of specific meetings, calls, workshops, reviews, or syncs.

Use this for:
- Meeting analysis
- Attendees
- Key decisions
- Action items
- Connections to projects/orgs/risks/commitments
- Candidate contributions
- Transcript or raw notes

Most important section:
- `Candidate Contributions`

Meeting pages are a primary source for:
- `state/decisions/`
- `state/commitments/`
- `state/actions/`
- `state/risks/`
- `contribution/candidates/`

Do not paste only AI meeting notes. Write what mattered, what changed, what was decided, and what needs follow-up.
