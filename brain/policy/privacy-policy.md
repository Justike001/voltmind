# Privacy Policy

Defines what must remain private.

Default private zones:
- `daily/`
- `private/`
- raw personal notes
- unreviewed meeting transcripts
- sensitive source excerpts

Rules:
- `private/` is never eligible for contribution.
- Raw `daily/` content is never published directly.
- Personal notes can only produce redacted `contribution/candidates/`.
- Restricted pages require explicit user approval before any sharing or writeback.
