# Risks

Risk objects.

Use this for:
- Personal suspected risks
- Team candidate risks
- Company candidate risks
- Published risks
- Blockers and concerns needing owner/evidence/mitigation

Every risk should include:
- Risk
- Impact
- Likelihood
- Related project/customer/system
- Evidence
- Owner
- Mitigation
- Scope
- Sensitivity

Rule:
- Personal Brain risks can be hypotheses.
- Company Brain risks require evidence, owner, and permissions.
